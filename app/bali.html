Tours to Indonesia to the island of Bali. Combined tours with Bali and other islands
The Island of the Gods, as the island of Bali is also called, is a small island in Indonesia, which belongs to the group of the Lesser Sunda Islands. Nearby lies the famous Wallace Line, a deep-sea depression that separates two completely different ecosystems of the world: tropical Asia and the natural system of Australia and New Guinea.

Unique in many respects, the island has become a favorite of travelers from all over the world for its unique culture and religion, developed infrastructure and incredible hospitality of local residents.

Tours and holidays in Bali in Indonesia for two, romantic tours, wedding ceremonies, wellness retreats and yoga tours, surf training, trekking to volcanoes, diving and snorkeling.
